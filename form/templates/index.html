Discipline Change Portal

Procedure :

The following is the procedure for change of discipline for students in Part-1 of their respective programs.

  • Change of discipline is subject to the eligibility and availability as described in the rules given below
    Students must read these rules and indicate their agreement by checking the box below.
  • Enter your official email (IIT-BHU) address in the text box and click submit. A link of the form to be filled is then sent to the given email address.
  • Use the link in the email to fill in the necessary details on the form and click submit. The completed form is then available for download in PDF format.
  • Download the completed form as a PDF file, sign it and hand it over in the office of the Assistant Registrar (Academic), IIT(BHU) (located in the Electrical Engineering building behind G10) on or before 20thApril,2015
